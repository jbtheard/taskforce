What we believe

  • We will back you only if we think we can quickly add value to your project.

  • We think we can be the most helpful for teams between ideas-stage to A-round.

  • We typically invest €30-50k, which is the right sum to bootstrap your project over the next 5-12 months.

  • We believe it's hard to build great things alone, so we are looking for fully dedicated teams of 2 to 4 people.
